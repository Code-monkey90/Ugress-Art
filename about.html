Ugress Art

About

Ugress is an abstract art project by Nora Hermine Tidemand-Johannessen. She is a self-thought painter, who started blending colours on canvas in January 2018.You can find me on:

‘I love mixing colours, finding different shades, and experiment with textures and techniques. It lets me express emotions in the moment I’m creating, using different tools, movements, and colours. When I paint, I’m able to let go of my usual structured way of seeing things, freed from confining patterns. I get to combine chaos and order, finding balance between the two. Most of the time I don’t have an idea of what I want to paint. Instead I focus on which colours and tools I want to start with. I let my hands flow, and my feelings decide.’
